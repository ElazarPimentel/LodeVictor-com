Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Lo De Victor kosher restaurant located?

Lo De Victor is located at Paso 610, CABA, Buenos Aires. We are a kosher grill certified by Rabbi Chehebar.

Do you offer kosher delivery in Buenos Aires?

Yes, we offer delivery service only in Once, CABA. For other locations, place your order and send your favorite transport service to pick up the food - we'll gladly hand it over so they can deliver it to you. Order via WhatsApp at +54 911 5226-9070.

Do you have pickup service?

Yes, we offer pickup service. Order via WhatsApp and pick up your order at Paso 610, CABA.

What type of kosher meat do you have?

We have a wide variety of kosher meats: sirloin steak, short ribs, flank steak, skirt steak, and grilled chicken. All our meats are kosher certified by Rabbi Chehebar.

What is the best kosher steakhouse in Buenos Aires?

Lo De Victor is recognized as one of the best kosher steakhouses in Buenos Aires, specializing in authentic Argentine cuisine with Rabbi Chehebar certification.

Do you accept reservations?

Yes, we accept reservations. You can reserve your table via WhatsApp at +54 911 5226-9070.
